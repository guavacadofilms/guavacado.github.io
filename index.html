about

Guavacado Films is an up-and-coming media production studio, specialising in filming and editing services. We provide multi-camera shoots with high-quality audio capture, whether a wedding or live music venue, and edit all content into any format required. This includes YouTube, Instagram, TikTok and more!


We are based in West London, however we are able to travel anywhere within the UK. We use our own equipment and edit using Davinci Resolve, but if you have your own equipment you would prefer us to use, that's absolutely fine! We also have proficiency in Avid, Premiere Pro and After Effects if you would prefer a certain program to be used.

contact us

Drop us an email at enquiries@guavacadofilms.com to bring your vision to life!
Pricing will be evaluated by gauging the scope of your project and your specific needs. Include as much detail as possible in your enquiry - this will help ensure we can tailor exactly to your requirements, and get started turning your vision into reality sooner! This is the service you can expect when you choose to work with us:

CONCEPTUALISATION

You give us the brief, we give you the plan of action. We'll outline a rough proposal involving equipment, logistics, locations and more. Whether a simple editing job or a full-scale documentary shoot, don't be afraid to delve into the details!

PRODUCTION

We begin the main event! From voice-overs to b-roll, and anything inbetween, we capture every moment you need to efficiently communicate your story.

POST-PRODUCTION

This is when your piece will be polished up in our state-of-the-art editing suite. This includes colour grading, transitions, music, VFX and more.

FINALISATION

Once a first draft is prepared, we send it over. This is your time to give us feedback and fine-tune the content to exactly what you have in mind. After implementing any changes, your video is ready to share with the world!
